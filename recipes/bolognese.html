Odin Bolognese Recipe

Description

Bolognese, originating from Bologna, Italy, is a meat-based sauce traditionally served with tagliatelle pasta. This rich, slow-cooked sauce combines ground beef, pancetta, and a soffritto of finely chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Tomato paste, red wine, and milk or cream are added to create depth of flavor, while beef broth helps achieve the right consistency. The sauce is simmered for several hours, resulting in a thick, savory coating that clings well to pasta. While authentically served with tagliatelle, it's often paired with spaghetti in many parts of the world outside Italy.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pancetta
  • Onions, carrots, celery
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine
