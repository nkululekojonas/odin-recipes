Cabonara Recipe

Desciption

Carbonara is a Roman pasta dish known for its creamy sauce and simple ingredients. It typically uses spaghetti or another long pasta, coated in a silky sauce made from raw eggs, hard cheese (usually Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), and black pepper. The sauce is enriched with small pieces of guanciale (cured pork cheek) or pancetta, which are crisped before being mixed with the pasta. The hot pasta is tossed with the egg and cheese mixture off the heat, creating a smooth, glossy coating without scrambling the eggs.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or other long pasta
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
  • Guanciale or pancetta
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
