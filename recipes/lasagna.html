Odins Lasagna

Description

A lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish consisting of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, savory fillings, and cheese, typically baked in an oven. Here's a brief description:

  1. Pasta layers: Made from durum wheat, these broad, flat noodles form the structure of the dish.
  2. Sauce: Usually a rich tomato-based sauce, often containing ground meat (beef or pork), though vegetarian versions exist.
  3. Cheese: Primarily uses mozzarella for its melting properties, and often includes ricotta and Parmesan.
  4. Filling variations: Can include vegetables, different meats, or seafood depending on the recipe.
  5. Texture: When cooked, it has a slightly crisp top layer, with soft, gooey inner layers.
  6. Flavor profile: Savory, with a balance of tangy tomato, creamy cheese, and herbs like basil and oregano.
  7. Appearance: When sliced, it reveals distinct layers, making for an visually appealing dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
